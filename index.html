About

Tom Alter as Ghalib

Originally based in Delhi,Pierrot's Troupe was founded in 1989 by Dr. M. Sayeed Alam.

Pierrot’s Troupe is an endeavor creatively expressed. Non-conformist yet rooted into the finest

traditions of Indian theatre, Pierrot’s Troupe is reputed as the “purveyor of original plays in India”.

Sons of Babur, Ghalib in New Delhi, A Private Affair,Rahe Naam, are some of the many critically acclaimed plays.

Pierrot's Troupe Mumbai

Pierrot's Troupe Mumbai is an extension of Pierrot's Troupe Delhi founded by Mr. Ajitabh Sengupta, who is

an actor and has appeared in many movies and web-series most notably in Netflix's "Yeh kaali kaali ankhein" ,

Scam 1992, movies like Omerta,Children of War, and many TV commercials,
